🔍 Key Clinical Insights — Dr. Sharma's Findings
Evidence-based recommendations for patient care and awareness campaigns
⚖️
BMI is a Critical Indicator
Obese patients (BMI 35+) have a 72% disease rate vs 9% for healthy weight. Weight management programs are the highest-ROI intervention.
🚬
Heavy Smoking Triples Risk
Heavy smokers face a 68% disease rate vs 14% for non-smokers. Smoking cessation counseling should be mandatory for all at-risk patients.
📈
Disease Peaks at 50–69
60% of diagnosed cases fall in the 50–69 age band. Proactive screening should begin at age 40 for high-risk individuals.
🏃
Daily Exercise Slashes Risk by 85%
Daily exercisers have only 11% disease rate vs 72% for sedentary patients. Structured exercise prescriptions must be integrated into care plans.
